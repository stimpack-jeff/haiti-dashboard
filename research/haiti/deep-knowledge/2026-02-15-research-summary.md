## 7. Updated Solutions Matrix Insights

### What Works (Evidence-Based):

**1. Social Urbanism (Medell√≠n Model) - üü¢ STRONG EVIDENCE**
- **Result:** 90% homicide reduction over 25 years (381/100k ‚Üí 20/100k)
- **Method:** Cable cars connecting poor hillside neighborhoods, libraries, parks, schools + security operations
- **Prerequisites:** Security baseline FIRST (gangs defeated/weakened), then 15-20 year sustained political commitment
- **Cost:** Billions over decades
- **Applicability to Haiti:** Excellent LONG-TERM model, but REQUIRES security phase completion first

**2. DDR (Liberia Model) - üü¢ STRONG EVIDENCE**
- **Result:** Disarmed 100,000+ former fighters (2003-2004), Liberia stable since
- **Prerequisites:** 
  - **War already ended** (ceasefire in place)
  - **UNMIL 15,000 UN troops** providing security guarantees
  - **Financial incentives** ($300/weapon)
  - **Reintegration programs** (vocational training, psychosocial support)
- **Applicability to Haiti:** ONLY works AFTER gangs militarily defeated - not while they're winning
- **Critical finding:** Reintegration is "Achilles Heel" of DDR - only minority sustainably reintegrated

**3. Mass Incarceration (El Salvador Model) - üü° MIXED EVIDENCE**
- **Result:** 80% homicide reduction in 3 years (short-term effective)
- **Costs:** 
  - ~50% wrongful detention rate (8,000+ innocent people imprisoned per Bukele admission)
  - 261 prison deaths in 2024 (torture, abuse)
  - Democratic backsliding (rule of law ranking fell to 111/142)
- **Prerequisites Haiti LACKS:**
  - Prison capacity (Haiti's prisons already gang-controlled)
  - Functional police force (Haiti has ~1,000 police for Port-au-Prince vs. 80,000+ gang members/sympathizers)
  - Authoritarian executive (Haiti has weak transitional government)
- **Verdict:** Could work in Haiti ONLY if preceded by prison-building + security sector reform + authoritarian leader (not currently available)

### What Doesn't Work (Evidence-Based):

**1. International Peacekeeping Without Political Reform (MINUSTAH Model) - üî¥ FAILED**
- 13 years (2004-2017), $7+ billion, gangs returned within 5 years of withdrawal
- Lesson: Security gains without addressing elite-gang nexus = temporary suppression, not resolution

**2. Gang Negotiation Without Security Pressure (El Salvador 2012 Truce) - üî¥ FAILED**
- Homicide reduction reversed when gangs reneged
- Gangs used truce to rearm and reorganize
