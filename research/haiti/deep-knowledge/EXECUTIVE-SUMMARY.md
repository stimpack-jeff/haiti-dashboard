## Key Insights (Most Important)

### 1. Haiti Must Build, Not Rebuild
No golden age to restore. Even "stable" periods were brief (4-13 years), externally dependent, ended in collapse. Aiming for something Haiti has never achieved: self-sustaining democratic stability.

### 2. No Single Model Works - Phased Hybrid Required
- **Phase 1 (Years 1-2):** Security - 15-20k international troops, targeted strikes, financial disruption
- **Phase 2 (Years 2-4):** DDR - Disarm gang members, rebuild police (Liberia model)
- **Phase 3 (Years 5-10):** Social Urbanism - Infrastructure, jobs, services (Medellín model)
- **Phase 4 (Years 10-20):** Sustainability - Transition to Haitian control

**Total Cost:** $8-13 billion over 20 years  
**Success Probability:** 40-50% (revised down after historical research)

### 3. Must Break Elite-Gang Nexus Immediately
Jamaica's failure shows: Security operations without breaking elite-gang ties = gangs reconstitute. Must freeze assets, sanction elite funders, prosecute collaborators from day one.

### 4. 20-Year Commitment Non-Negotiable
Medellín: 25 years. Rwanda: 15+ years. Haiti never sustained 30+ years of stability. Shorter timelines are fantasy. International community must commit or don't start.

### 5. External Shocks Are Constant
Haiti gets major hurricanes/earthquakes every 5-10 years. 2010 earthquake destroyed progress. Must build resilience + rapid recovery into strategy.

## What Could Go Wrong

**High-Risk Scenarios:**
1. **Phase 1 violence spike** - Urban warfare causes civilian casualties, international backlash, withdrawal
2. **Elite sabotage** - Haitian oligarchs undermine interventions (Jamaica repeat)
3. **Gang adaptation** - Gangs go underground, wait out intervention, return later
4. **International fatigue** - Donors pull funding after 5 years (MINUSTAH repeat)
5. **Natural disaster** - Hurricane/earthquake during critical phase (2010 repeat)
6. **Political collapse** - Haiti government can't transition from transitional council

**Mitigation:** Build redundancy, over-communicate with donors, target elites immediately, plan for setbacks
