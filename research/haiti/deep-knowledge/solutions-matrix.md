## What Could Go Wrong?

**High Risk Scenarios:**
1. **Phase 1 violence spike** - Urban warfare causes civilian casualties, international backlash
2. **Elite sabotage** - Haitian oligarchs undermine interventions (see Jamaica)
3. **Gang adaptation** - Gangs go underground, wait out intervention
4. **International fatigue** - Donors pull funding after 5 years (MINUSTAH repeat)
5. **Regional instability** - DR conflict, Venezuela collapse spills over
6. **Natural disaster** - Hurricane/earthquake during critical phase
7. **Political collapse** - Haitian government can't transition from transitional council

**Mitigation:**
- Build redundancy into each phase
- Over-communicate with donors (maintain support)
- Target elites from day one (can't let them sabotage)
- Plan for setbacks (assume 2-3 major crises over 20 years)
